Teeth Cleaning & Whitening

Professional scaling, polishing, and laser whitening to remove plaque, tartar, and deep stains, giving you a brighter, healthier smile in a single visit.

1-Visit Laser Whitening Plaque & Tartar Removal No Recovery Time Enamel-Safe Treatments
Overview

About Teeth Cleaning & Whitening

Professional teeth cleaning (scaling) uses ultrasonic instruments to remove hardened plaque and tartar (calculus) from above and below the gumline — deposits that regular brushing cannot clean. This is followed by polishing to remove external stains. Teeth whitening (bleaching) uses a professional-grade peroxide gel activated by a special light to break down deep stains within the enamel structure.

At Grace Dental Care, we provide both treatments. Regular scaling is essential every 6 months to prevent gum disease and bad breath. Our in-office laser whitening is safe, fast, and highly effective — brightening your smile by up to 8 shades in a single 60-minute session without damaging the enamel.

Indications

Signs You May Need This Treatment

If you identify with any of the following, a clinical consultation is strongly recommended.

Yellow or Discoloured TeethTeeth losing their natural brightness due to age, food, or lifestyle habits.
Plaque and Tartar Build-upHard, rough yellow or brown deposits near the gumline that brushing cannot remove.
Persistent Bad BreathChronic halitosis often caused by bacteria living in tartar deposits.
Bleeding or Swollen GumsGums bleeding during brushing — an early sign of gingivitis caused by tartar.
Coffee, Tea, or Smoking StainsDark surface stains accumulated over years of beverage consumption or smoking.
Self-Consciousness when SmilingAvoiding open smiles due to stains, plaque, or tooth discolouration.
The Process

Step-by-Step Procedure

What to expect at every stage of your treatment journey.

1
Dental Examination & Shade Guide Check
We examine your teeth and gums to ensure they are healthy. We record your initial shade using a dental shade guide.
⏱ 10 mins · Visit 1
2
Ultrasonic Scaling & Plaque Removal
An ultrasonic scaler uses micro-vibrations and water spray to gently break and remove plaque and hardened tartar from all tooth surfaces.
⏱ 25 mins
3
Teeth Polishing & Stain Erasure
A prophylactic paste and a rotating rubber cup are used to polish the enamel surface, removing superficial stains and leaving teeth smooth.
⏱ 15 mins
4
Gingival Barrier & Gel Application
For whitening, a protective liquid dam is applied to cover the gums, and the medical-grade whitening gel is painted onto the teeth.
⏱ 15 mins
5
Laser Activation & Final Reveal
A special blue laser light activates the whitening gel for 15-minute intervals. The gel is rinsed off, and the new shade is recorded.
⏱ 30–45 mins

Eligibility

Am I a Candidate?

Ideal Candidates

  • Adults with external stains (coffee, tea, wine, smoking)
  • Those with yellowing teeth due to ageing
  • Patients with plaque, tartar, and early gum inflammation
  • Individuals preparing for a wedding, interview, or event

Pre-treatment / Not Suitable if…

  • Children under 16 (pulp chambers are large and sensitive)
  • Severe tooth sensitivity or worn-out enamel
  • Active cavities, exposed roots, or advanced gum disease
  • Pregnant or lactating mothers (as a safety precaution)

Recovery

Aftercare & Recovery Tips

Following these instructions carefully will maximize your treatment success and speed up healing.

1
Follow the "White Diet"For 48 hours post-whitening, avoid dark foods/drinks: coffee, tea, wine, colas, soy sauce, curries.
2
Avoid Smoking & TobaccoNicotine will quickly discolour freshly whitened teeth as the enamel pores are temporarily open.
3
Use Sensitive ToothpasteMild sensitivity for 24–48 hours is normal. Brush with a desensitising toothpaste.
4
Brush and Floss DailyPrevent plaque accumulation by brushing twice daily and flossing once daily.
5
Rinse After Staining FoodsIf you drink coffee or tea, rinse your mouth with water immediately after to prevent staining.
6
Visit Every 6 MonthsSchedule scaling and polishing every 6 months to maintain health and color.
