Braces & Aligners

Modern orthodontic treatment to align teeth and correct bite — using clear aligners or ceramic braces for a beautifully straight smile at any age.

Clear Aligners Available Custom 3D Treatment Plan Visits Every 4–6 Weeks 8–24 Months Duration
Overview

About Braces & Aligners

Orthodontic treatment corrects misaligned teeth and bite problems using controlled forces applied over time. Modern options include clear aligner systems (removable, nearly invisible) and ceramic self-ligating braces that are far more comfortable and discreet than traditional metal braces. Treatment begins with digital 3D scanning to map your teeth precisely and simulate the end result.

At Grace Dental Care our orthodontist creates a personalised treatment plan for each patient. Clear aligners are worn 20-22 hours per day and changed every 1-2 weeks as teeth progressively move. Ceramic braces use tooth-coloured brackets that blend with enamel — effective for moderate to severe misalignment cases.

Indications

Signs You May Need This Treatment

If you identify with any of the following, a clinical consultation is strongly recommended.

Crooked or Crowded TeethTeeth that overlap or are rotated due to lack of space in the jaw.
Gaps Between TeethSpaces or diastemas between teeth that affect appearance and trap food.
Overbite / UnderbiteUpper teeth overlapping lower teeth excessively, or lower teeth protruding forward.
Crossbite or Open BiteTeeth not meeting properly when biting, causing wear or speech issues.
Difficulty ChewingMisaligned bite making it hard or uncomfortable to chew certain foods.
Jaw Pain or ClickingBite issues putting stress on the temporomandibular joint (TMJ).
The Process

Step-by-Step Procedure

What to expect at every stage of your treatment journey.

1
Orthodontic Consultation & 3D Scanning
We take digital photos, X-rays, and a 3D intraoral scan of your teeth. Our orthodontist evaluates your bite and bone structure.
⏱ 45 mins · Visit 1
2
Digital Treatment Simulation
We generate a 3D digital plan showing the precise movement of your teeth and a simulation of your final smile before treatment begins.
⏱ 30 mins · Visit 2
3
Braces Placement or Aligner Delivery
For braces, brackets are bonded to teeth. For aligners, custom trays are delivered and attachments are placed on teeth to guide movements.
⏱ 60–90 mins · Visit 3
4
Periodic Adjustment Visits
You visit us every 4–6 weeks for braces adjustments or progress checks and receiving new sets of aligner trays.
⏱ 20 mins · Every 4–6 weeks
5
Retention Phase
After teeth are aligned, braces are removed. A custom retainer (fixed or removable) is fitted to prevent teeth from shifting back.
⏱ 30 mins · Final Visit

Eligibility

Am I a Candidate?

Ideal Candidates

  • Teens and adults with crowded or crooked teeth
  • Those with gaps or spacing issues between teeth
  • Patients with overbite, underbite, crossbite, or open bite
  • Active individuals looking for clear aligners
  • A medically fit mouth with healthy gums and bone support

Pre-treatment / Not Suitable if…

  • Active, untreated gum disease or cavities
  • Severe skeletal jaw discrepancies (may require jaw surgery)
  • Poor compliance (reluctant to wear aligners 22 hours/day)
  • Aged under 7 (first orthodontic evaluation is recommended at age 7)

Recovery

Aftercare & Recovery Tips

Following these instructions carefully will maximize your treatment success and speed up healing.

1
Brush After Every MealFood particles easily trap around braces and brackets. Brush thoroughly after eating.
2
Clean Aligners DailyWash clear aligners with lukewarm water and a soft brush. Never use hot water.
3
Wear Aligners 22 Hours/DayRemove aligners only for eating, drinking hot liquids, brushing, and flossing.
4
Avoid Hard & Sticky FoodsFor braces, stay away from nuts, hard candy, popcorn, gums, and sticky sweets.
5
Wear Retainers DiligentlyTeeth have "memory" and will drift back. Wear your retainers exactly as prescribed.
6
Maintain Regular Dental CleaningProfessional scaling every 6 months is crucial to keep gums healthy during treatment.
