About Dr. Indumathiy

Gentle Care,
Lasting Results

Dr. Indumathiy R completed her Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) with a focus on restorative and preventive dentistry. Since joining Grace Dental Care, she has become the cornerstone of the clinic's day-to-day patient care, handling everything from routine cleaning appointments to complex restorative cases.

What sets Dr. Indumathiy apart is her extraordinary ability to put patients at ease. Many adults carry deep anxiety about dental treatment from childhood experiences. She approaches these patients with exceptional patience, explaining every step in plain Tamil or English, always making sure they feel in control of their own care journey.

Her particular area of interest is preventive and paediatric dentistry. She believes that instilling good oral health habits in children early sets them up for a lifetime of healthy smiles. She conducts age-appropriate dental hygiene education at the clinic, making learning fun and memorable for young patients.

"My six-year-old was terrified of dentists. Dr. Indu sat with him for fifteen minutes just talking about dinosaurs before even picking up a tool. He now actually looks forward to his check-ups."

— Priya S., Parent of a Paediatric Patient

In restorative work, Dr. Indumathiy applies meticulous technique to tooth-coloured fillings, crowns, and root canal procedures. She employs digital X-rays and rotary endodontic systems to ensure precision, minimal discomfort, and efficient recovery for every patient.

She is a member of the Indian Dental Association (IDA) and actively participates in continuing dental education programmes to keep her skills current with the latest evidence-based techniques.
