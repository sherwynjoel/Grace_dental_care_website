Our Story

Born from a Passion
for Exceptional Care

Grace Dental Care was founded on a simple but powerful belief: every patient — regardless of age or dental condition — deserves compassionate, world-class dental treatment delivered by specialists who genuinely care.

Dr. Sherin Grace Babu established the clinic in Kovaipudur, Coimbatore, envisioning a practice that would combine specialist expertise with the warmth of a family clinic. Over the years, that vision has blossomed into one of Coimbatore's most trusted dental destinations.

Today, we serve patients not only from across Coimbatore but also from Pollachi, Palakkad, and even international patients who travel specifically for our care. Our ground-floor, fully accessible clinic ensures that no patient — young or senior, differently-abled or anxious — ever feels unwelcome.
