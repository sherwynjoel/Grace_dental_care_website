About Dr. Nandana

Compassionate Care,
Artistic Precision

Dr. Nandana Jayachandran completed her Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) from Annoor Dental College and Hospital, Muvattupuzha, affiliated with the Kerala University of Health and Sciences. She completed her intensive clinical internship between October 2023 and November 2024, followed by valuable clinical experience at Dr. Varun's Smile World Dental Clinic, where she honed her skills in diverse aspects of general dentistry.

She is passionate about making dental visits comfortable and anxiety-free. By focusing on empathetic patient interactions and clear communication, she ensures that patients are active partners in their own oral health journey, explaining procedures step-by-step and helping them feel entirely at ease.

With a natural eye for detail and an artistic approach, Dr. Nandana excels in restorative treatments, including composite, GIC, and amalgam restorations, as well as crown preparation. She is also skilled in pediatric dental care, tooth extractions, and the design and fitting of complete and removable partial dentures.

"Dr. Nandana was incredibly patient and gentle. She took the time to explain the procedure and did a fantastic job with my filling. I felt completely comfortable."

— Ananth R., General Dentistry Patient

Dr. Nandana is dedicated to continuous learning and staying updated with modern dental practices. She has attended numerous specialized workshops, including hands-on programs in crown preparation, root canal therapy, and intraoral scanners, enabling her to offer evidence-based, modern care to every patient.
