About Dr. Sherin

Clinical Philosophy &
Approach to Care

Dr. Sherin Grace Babu completed her Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) with distinction, followed by a Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) specialising in Prosthodontics and Crown & Bridge — one of the most technically demanding dental specialities.

Her philosophy is rooted in personalised care: no two patients are the same, and neither should their treatment be. Before recommending any procedure, Dr. Sherin takes the time to thoroughly understand each patient's dental history, lifestyle, aesthetic goals, and anxieties. Only then does she design a treatment plan that is truly tailored.

A particular passion of hers is the transformative power of implant dentistry. "Losing a tooth affects far more than your bite," she explains. "It affects confidence, speech, nutrition, and the way you engage with the world. Restoring that is deeply rewarding." She has placed over 1,000 implants using the latest Nobel Biocare and Straumann systems, with a documented success rate above 98%.

"I was terrified of dentists after a bad experience. Dr. Sherin took so much time to explain everything before touching a single tooth. I walked out with six implants and zero regrets."

— Meena K., Full Arch Implant Patient

Beyond clinical work, Dr. Sherin is a strong advocate for dental literacy in the community. She regularly hosts patient education sessions at Grace Dental Care, demystifying complex procedures and encouraging preventive care from an early age.

She is a life member of the Indian Dental Association (IDA) and has completed advanced training in digital smile design, guided implant surgery, and laser dentistry through programmes in India and abroad.
