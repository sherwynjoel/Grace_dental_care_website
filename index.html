About Grace Dental Care

A Decade of Transforming
Smiles & Lives

Founded by Dr. Sherin Grace Babu, Grace Dental Care has been Kovaipudur's most trusted dental clinic for over a decade. Our philosophy is simple — every patient deserves the highest standard of care in a warm, stress-free environment.

We are a true one-stop clinic: specialist doctors handle each procedure, so you receive the right expertise for every treatment. From routine cleanings to complex full-mouth rehabilitations, we do it all with precision and compassion.

  • Specialist doctor for every procedure
  • Ground-floor clinic — fully wheelchair accessible
  • International patients welcome — online consultations available
  • Ample parking, convenient 100 Feet Road location
