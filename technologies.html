Technology 01

Digital Chairside X-Ray

Our digital X-ray system delivers instant, crystal-clear images at 90% less radiation than traditional film X-rays. We place the sensor chairside — no dark rooms, no waiting, no unnecessary exposure.

90% less radiation than conventional X-ray
Instant results — no waiting for development
High-resolution images for precise diagnosis
Technology 02

Full-Mouth Digital Scanning

Say goodbye to uncomfortable physical impressions. Our digital scanner creates a precise 3D map of your mouth in minutes — no mess, no gagging, perfect accuracy for crowns, braces, and implant planning.

No physical impressions — completely comfortable
3D model ready in minutes
Superior accuracy for better fitting restorations
Technology 03

Laser Dentistry

Our dental laser offers a revolutionary approach to gum treatment, cavity detection, and soft tissue procedures. Compared to conventional scalpel surgery, laser treatment means less bleeding, faster healing, and dramatically less discomfort.

Virtually painless gum treatment
Minimal bleeding and faster recovery
Used for gum disease, frenectomy, depigmentation
Technology 04

Digital Smile Design

Before a single tooth is touched, our Digital Smile Design software lets you preview your new smile. We map your facial proportions, tooth dimensions, and aesthetic goals — creating a digital mockup you approve first. No surprises, only delight.

See your new smile before treatment starts
Co-create your ideal result with your doctor
Guides precise fabrication of veneers and crowns
